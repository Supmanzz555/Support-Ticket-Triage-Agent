# Account and Plans

## Plan Overview

### Free Plan
- Basic access to core features
- Limited exports and storage
- No dark mode
- Best for trying the product

### Pro Plan
- Full access to export features (PDF, high-res)
- Dark mode included
- More storage and collaboration options
- Ideal for individuals and small teams

### Enterprise Plan
- Everything in Pro plus SSO, SLAs, dedicated support
- Regional deployment options
- Priority handling for outages and issues
- For organizations with higher compliance and uptime needs

## How to Check Your Plan

1. Go to Settings > Account (or Profile)
2. Your current plan is shown at the top under "Subscription" or "Plan"
3. You will see: Free, Pro, or Enterprise
4. Tenure (how long you have been a customer) is visible in Account > Billing history

If you are unsure, you can say: "You're currently on the [plan] plan. You can confirm this in Settings > Account."

- Pro and Enterprise can downgrade to Free from Settings > Billing
- Refunds follow our refund policy (see Billing article)
