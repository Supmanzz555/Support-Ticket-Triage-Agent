# Billing and Payment Issues

## Payment Failures

### Common Causes
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired or invalid
- Bank security blocks
- Billing address mismatch

### Resolution Steps
1. Verify card details are correct
2. Check with bank for any security blocks
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact billing support if issue persists

### Multiple Charges
If a customer sees multiple pending charges:
- Charges are typically temporary authorizations
- They will be automatically reversed if the transaction fails
- Refunds process within 3-5 business days
- Contact billing immediately if charges persist beyond 5 days

### Upgrade Issues
If upgrade payment succeeds but account doesn't update:
- Account updates can take 5-10 minutes
- Clear browser cache and refresh
- Log out and log back in
- Contact support if issue persists after 15 minutes

## Refund Policy
- Full refunds available within 30 days
- Pro-rated refunds for annual plans
- Contact billing@company.com for refund requests
